Coaching Training
Data-Driven Analysis for Strategic Planning
A professional development workshop for UA Capstone Center staff, designed during a period of rapid organizational growth to build internal capacity for data collection, analysis, and strategic decision-making. Built on ADDIE, with department-specific breakout discussions and guided SWOT analysis practice, with an explicit goal: equip staff to independently own and develop new initiatives using data, rather than relying on top-down direction.
ADDIE Framework Data Literacy Design Performance Gap Analysis SWOT Facilitation SME Consultation Strategic Planning
1
The Need
Rapid organizational growth outpacing staff capacity for data-driven decision-making

During a period of significant program expansion, internal staff lacked the foundational knowledge and practical experience to collect, process, and analyze data effectively. Inefficient data and planning procedures were frustrating employees and threatening to stall organizational momentum. The need was both a knowledge gap and a performance gap: the organization could not scale sustainably if strategic planning continued to depend on top-down direction rather than distributed data ownership.

2
The Learner
Professional staff across departments, with varying levels of data experience

Internal staff at the Capstone Center for Student Success, spanning multiple departments and a wide range of comfort with data analysis. This session was built specifically for professional staff rather than peer coaches, reflecting the more complex, initiative-ownership expectations placed on the full-time team.

3
The Approach
ADDIE-framed, department-specific, closed with a live SWOT practice

Used ADDIE as the design framework and consulted SMEs to ensure content accuracy and departmental relevance. Built as a synchronous, interactive workshop with visual learning checks to reinforce foundational data concepts, department-specific breakout discussions connecting abstract concepts to each team's real work, and brainstorming sessions for data visualization and stakeholder engagement. Closed with guided SWOT analysis practice, giving staff an immediately applicable strategic planning tool they could carry into their next initiative.
